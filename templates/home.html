Flood Prediction

About the Project

Floods are among the most destructive natural disasters, often causing significant damage to lives, property, and the environment. Rising Waters is an intelligent Machine Learning-based flood prediction system developed to provide early flood risk assessment using historical weather data. By analyzing rainfall patterns, temperature, humidity, cloud cover, and seasonal rainfall, the system identifies potential flood conditions with high accuracy. The application delivers fast and reliable predictions through a simple web interface, helping disaster management authorities, government agencies, and local communities make informed decisions and take timely preventive measures before flood events occur.

🌦 Weather Intelligence

The system continuously analyzes critical weather parameters, including rainfall, temperature, humidity, cloud cover, and seasonal rainfall patterns. These environmental indicators help identify conditions that may lead to flooding and improve the accuracy of flood risk assessment.

🧠 Smart AI Prediction

Advanced Machine Learning algorithms including Decision Tree, Random Forest, K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN), and XGBoost analyze historical weather records to recognize hidden patterns and generate accurate flood predictions. The best-performing model is integrated into the application for reliable real-time forecasting.

🚨 Instant Results

Users simply enter the required weather parameters into the prediction form and receive an instant flood risk prediction within seconds. The easy-to-use interface enables quick analysis and supports timely decision-making for disaster preparedness and emergency response.

Developed using Python | Flask | Scikit-learn | Decision Tree | Random Forest | KNN | XGBoost
